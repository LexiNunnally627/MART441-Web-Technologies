Gaming Memory

I have lots of different memories surrounding games. But one that stands out the most recently is when my mom and I were playing Uno. But not just any type of Uno, Uno No Mercy. The game adds cards to the classic game that make it nearly impossible to win. Of course there is a way, it just might take a while. At one point I had about 30 cards in my hand; however, that allowed me to pick up a bunch of draw 10 cards. So I used a bunch of my wild draw cards, skips, and reverses all in a row. I thought it was funny, but I don't think my mom did. Oh well. Thats all a part of the game. I would highly recommend grabbing a copy of this version of Uno. It was so much fun and I'm sure it would be more fun with more people. Buy Uno No Mercy
